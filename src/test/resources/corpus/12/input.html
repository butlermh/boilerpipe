Common Talks New Mixtape, LUV & More w/ DJ Whoo Kid | Video

Chicago native MC, Common, recently sat down with DJ Whoo Kid for a little discussion about new music, acting, and what kind of roles he likes to take; amongst other things. The get into details about Common’s upcoming film, LUV, which is due out very soon.

But the real details come when the fellas discuss Common’s upcoming mixtape. While speaking about the project, the fellas speak about the production, which will be held down by No I.D. and more. Sounds dope right? Check out the clip after the jump and let us know what you think.
